User Guide
1 · What Is My BIO Performance©?
My BIO Performance© is a personal tool that allows the user to capture behavioral patterns that can affect a specific area of focus in our lives and create events, negative or positive, that the user may want to change or replicate. The tool allows the user to:
1. Track daily life events across various categories.
2. Define and score your focal performance events based on an area of focus (exercise, sickness etc.).
3. Use AI technology to surface patterns (positive or negative) in your food, sleep, stress, add-ons, supplements, and activity data that lead into a specific event or condition that you define and provide suggestions of likely contributors.
Using this pattern data, a person can consider adjustments in their routines to either change the experienced event, enhance or repeat it.
Important: My BIO Performance© is NOT intended to diagnose or suggest expertise or a treatment method for any area of focus, mental or physical condition being experienced and/or tracked by the user. While Artificial Intelligence data is pulled from a variety of large reliable data sources, it is still limited and should be verified with a subject matter expert.

All entered data is stored privately on your device. No account, no login, no data sent anywhere outside of the application except when the user explicitly requests an AI analysis or sync with an outside service.
2 · Initial Setup (Do This First)
Step 1 — Area of Focus
Foundation → Set-Up. Type your Area of Focus (Examples: strength training, managing anxiety, improving sleep, gut health issue, weight loss). This is injected into every AI analysis for personalized recommendations.
Step 2 — Experience Range
Allows the user to define 3 areas of measurement based on how you perceive an experience. Set the score thresholds for OK / Good / Strong in Set-Up. A common starting split is 7 / 7 / 6 (out of 20 total). Adjust over time as you calibrate.
OK = baseline · Good = above average · Strong = peak performance
Step 3 — Add Supplements
Foundation → Supplements. Add each supplement with name, purpose, serving size, quantity by taking a picture of the product label with your mobile device camera or pull an image from your stored picture gallery.
Step 4 — Connect Strava (optional)
Foundation → Set-Up → Connect Strava. Your activities will appear in Log Entries for one-tap import as scored Focal Events.
Step 5 — Take a Backup
Foundation → Set-Up → Backup Data. This saves your data. It is your only recovery option if you clear browser data or switch devices. We suggest a recurring calendar entry to remind you.
3 · Hourly/Daily Behavioral Logging
Tap Log Entries at the top. Select a category, write or verbalize your note, and tap Log Entry. Try to be clear and specific as this will help the AI analysis to be more effective.
Categories to log:
◑ Sleep & Recovery
Bedtime, wake time, HRV etc.
◆ Food & Nutrition
Meals, timing, gut reactions etc.
◎ Stress & Mental
Mood, anxiety, mental load etc.
◈ Recovery Model
Rate muscle soreness, mental & physical recovery
▲ Focal Events
Workouts, races, emotional low, physical discomfort etc. — scored 3–15
Scoring Focal Events
Rate three dimensions 1–5: Strength · Endurance · Mental State. Total score 3–15 is matched against your experience ranges.
4 · Behavioral Analysis
Seven AI-powered report cards are provided. Data outcomes depend on the user's entered content. Reports auto-refresh in the background on app open (cache older than 6 hours). Tap ⓘ on any report to expand the full category breakdown.
1. Supplement Review List  ·  instant list, no AI
2. Supplement Analysis  ·  ingredient stack + optimal timing
3. Food & Nutrition  ·  food, snacks, gut, sugar, lipids, food pyramid
4. Sleep & Recovery  ·  timing, consistency
5. Stress & Mental  ·  morning / afternoon / evening patterns
6. Recovery Model  ·  recovery patterns & impact signals
7. Focal Events  ·  select a tier, analyze 5 most recent
5 · Pattern Discovery
Cross-references a specific performance event against behavioral data leading up to it. The number of days to be included is based on the "Pattern Discovery Window" defined in setup.
1. Select experience tier (OK / Good / Strong)
2. Pick one of the 4 most recent matching Focal Events
3. Tap Analyze This Event
4. Read the day3 → day2 → day1 grid — rows = report card elements, columns = days before
Strong = supporting performance  ·  Moderate = mixed  ·  Needs Work = limiting

7 · Tips for Best Results
· The AI engines need data so be consistent. Log every day, even briefly — one note per category beats nothing.
· Be specific in notes — the AI reads them verbatim
· Calibrate ranges — if everything scores Strong, widen the buckets
· Back up weekly — the backup file is your only safety net
· Check Pattern Discovery after your 5th–10th scored Focal Event
· Update Area of Focus when and if your goals change
· Run Behavioral Analysis after a week of logging to prime the cache
